City Council Passes New Environmental Initiatives

The City Council has approved a series of initiatives aimed at reducing emissions and promoting sustainable practices across the community. The policies include expanded recycling programs, incentives for green businesses, and investments in public transportation. Supporters hope these measures will set an example for other cities.

Local School Launches Coding Program for Students

Greenwood High School has launched a new coding program for students, aiming to equip them with skills for the digital age. The program includes classes in Python, JavaScript, and web development, with plans to introduce robotics in the coming months. Students have expressed enthusiasm, seeing it as a pathway to future tech careers.
